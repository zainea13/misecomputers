About Us

M.I.S.E. Computer Store is a licensed computer brand based in Cleveland, Ohio, USA. Founded in 2025 by four computer geniuses — Ian, Maureen, Selena, and Emmanuel — all graduates of Cleveland Code Academy, the company was born out of a vision to provide the community with access to high-quality, genuine computers at affordable prices.

M.I.S.E. Computer Store specializes in both laptops and desktop computers, making technology accessible to individuals, students, businesses, and organizations without compromising quality.

The company is a licensed partner with top global technology brands, including:

  • Apple
  • ASUS
  • Dell
  • HP
  • Lenovo
Through these partnerships, M.I.S.E. Computer Store guarantees original, durable, and reliable products directly from manufacturers.

Our Mission:

To bridge the gap between affordability and quality by offering genuine computer products at unbeatable prices, thereby empowering individuals and businesses in our community.

Our Vision:

To rival retail giants like Amazon and Walmart by focusing on customer trust, affordability, and guaranteed authenticity in every product we sell.
